# Brand Persona: Sher’s Café

### Personality & Tone
**Urban Chic & Warmth**
Sher’s Café is sophisticated yet approachable. It blends high-end specialty coffee culture with the cozy feel of a local neighborhood spot. The vibe is clean, modern, and aesthetically "Instagrammable," emphasizing craftsmanship and quality.

### Color Palette
* **Deep Charcoal (#2B2D42):** Used for high-contrast typography and sleek UI elements.
* **Honey Gold (#D4A373):** Used for action buttons, icons, and highlights.
* **Soft Linen (#EDF2F4):** Used as the primary background for a clean, airy feel.

### Typography Suggestions (Google Fonts)
* **Headings:** Playfair Display (Bold) – An elegant serif font that conveys an artisanal feel.
* **Body:** Lato (Regular) – A clean, modern sans-serif font for maximum readability.

### Marketing Pitch
"Sher’s Café is where urban rhythm meets the perfect brew, offering a sanctuary for those who value artisanal quality and aesthetic comfort. Transform your daily caffeine ritual into an inspired moment of calm in the heart of the city."
